About Us

Operated by Bro. Scott Jacobs and his wife Cathi Jacobs, Central Christian Academy exists to provide a safe learning environment for students in Spring Branch and surrounding areas. Since it's opening, CCA has not only educated thousands of students from kindergarten to 12th grade, it has fostered a rich Christian environment to instill life-long values in all children who pass through its doors. Our talented staff is committed to helping students set goals and reach their full potential.
