CCA ADMISSIONS

Registration

*Registration is required each year.

   -Early Registration (by July 1st) $175.00
   -Registration (after July 1st) $200.00.

*If you register before July 2nd, there is a $25.00 discount per child in each family. Download School Supply List or Student Application

Books

Books are purchased each year.

*Students will work out of these books throughout the school year.
*Prices range from $125 for pre-K to $325 for high school.

Tuition

*Pre-K through 5th Grade: $3,500.00/year or 10 payments of $350.00/month
*6th through 12th Grade: $3,850.00/year or 10 payments of $385.00/month

Fees

*Each Sport: $75.00
*Fundraisers: $200.00 minimum

Lunch

*Students may bring lunches and snacks from home or purchase hot lunches, sandwiches, and snacks from the lunchroom.
